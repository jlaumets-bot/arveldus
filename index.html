Frequently asked

Common questions

Is it really free?
Yes. Arvik is fully free right now — no limits on invoices, clients, or PDF downloads. A paid plan with team features may come later, but current use stays free forever.
Where is my data stored?
By default, in your browser's local storage — it never leaves your device. If you turn on sync, data is encrypted in your browser before being sent to the cloud — the server only sees ciphertext that can't be opened without your code. Encryption uses AES-256-GCM and the key is derived from your code via PBKDF2 (120,000 iterations).
Can I use it on multiple devices?
Yes. Activate sync on one device — you get a QR code and a share link. Scan the QR with your phone camera and tap the link, or paste the share link into another browser. Nothing to type. Data is encrypted on your device before being sent — we never see it. Keep the link safe — losing it means losing access to the synced data.
Is there VAT support?
Yes. Add your company's VAT number and your client's VAT number. The VAT rate is editable on every invoice (default 22%, change to whatever your country uses). VAT and registration number show on the PDF following standard invoice format.
How do I send the invoice to my client?
Click "Save & Download PDF" — the file downloads instantly. Then attach it to an email from your normal email client (Gmail, Outlook). We keep it intentionally simple: your email stays under your control.
Can I add my logo and IBAN?
Of course. Settings → upload a logo (PNG or JPG), fill in your company info, bank and IBAN. Everything shows on every invoice PDF automatically.
